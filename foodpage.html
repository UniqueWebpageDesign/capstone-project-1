INDIAN CUISINE

Indian cuisine consists of variety of regional and traditional cuisines native to the Indian subcontinent. Given the diversity in soil,climate,,culture, ethnic groups, and occupations,these cuisines vary substantially and use locally available spices,herbs,vegetables and fruits.Staple foods of Indain Cuisine include pearl millet,rice,wheat and variety of lentils. Indian dishes are cooked in vegetable oil and ghee.Fish and meat are also consumed in some parts of India.
